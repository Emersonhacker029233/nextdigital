About Us

NextDigital Limited was founded in 2011 by a group of Tanzanian ICT Experts and Entrepreneurs, who have a significant number of years of experience in the Information Communication and Telecommunications industry. NextDigital Limited is a Company Limited by Shares and registered in Tanzania by the Business Registration and Licensing Agency (BRELA). NextDigital Limited is fully owned and operated by Tanzanians.

NextDigital Limited is attentive of the growing requirement for an efficient and effective system of supplying quality products and professional services. We endeavor to offer comprehensive solutions, technical support, service and warranty, to help our clients truly benefit from using our products, services and solutions.
