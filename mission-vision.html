Mission & Vision

- To be the premier source of Information and Communication Technologies products, services and solutions by delivering outstanding products and services. In so doing, we will create a company synonymous with quality, creativity, professionalism and excellence thus allowing us to succeed where others have failed and prosper as a business.

Goals

To bring in innovative technology to deliver value adds business improvements.
To provide IT solutions to address the specific business challenges of the client with on site project management and local support.
To distinguish ourselves with the customer by maintaining consistent standards of support and service levels.
Continuously improve our overall delivery capabilities through staff motivation, skill sets upgrade, training and professional work environment.

About Us

Next Digital Limited was founded in 2011 by a group of Tanzanian ICT Experts and Entrepreneurs, who have a significant number of years of experience in the Information Communication and Telecommunications industry. NextDigital Limited is a Company Limited by Shares and registered in Tanzania by the Business Registration and Licensing Agency (BRELA). NextDigital Limited is fully owned and operated by Tanzanians.
